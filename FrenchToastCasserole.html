French Toast Casserole

Baked, custardy, cinnamon-spiced breakfast favorite

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup chopped pecans (optional)
  • Powdered sugar and maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish and place bread cubes evenly inside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cream, granulated sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt until well combined.
  3. Pour egg mixture evenly over bread cubes, pressing down lightly to help bread absorb custard. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  5. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, brown sugar, and pecans. Sprinkle evenly over casserole.
  6. Bake uncovered for 45–50 minutes, or until golden and set.
  7. Cool slightly before serving. Dust with powdered sugar and drizzle with maple syrup.

Quick Info

Serves: 8–10

Prep time: 15 mins (plus chilling)

Cook time: 50 mins

Best served warm with coffee or fresh fruit.
